Berlin Expert Days 2025

Lightweight reporting architecture, inspired by CQRS

At BED-Con, a variety of software and tech topics are presented, ranging from software architecture, AI, security to digital sovereignty and social topics. I was invited to present the software architecture of a reporting domain at Jungheinrich Digital Solutions and its iterative implementation. The idea is inspired by Command Query Responsibility Segregation (CQRS) without introducing complexity of distributed systems.

Joint International Conference on Multibody System Dynamics 2018

partsival – Collision-based Particle and many-body Simulations on GPUs for Planetary Exploration Systems

Based on my master thesis , my supervisor and I published a paper at the IMSD in 2018 in Lisbon, Portugal. The paper and talk covered some applications of the partsival simulation tool as well as additional proves of its correctness and performance.
