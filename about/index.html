About me

On this page, you can finde some infromation about me.

My name is Simon Kerler alias namelessvoid. I study computer science at the University of Applied Sciences, Augsburg.

As long as I can remember, I have been interested in computers, espacially gaming, but also in programming and creating games. Therefore, I wanted to study computer science early on.

In 2011, I acquired my higher education entrance qualification at Ringeisen Gymnasium Ursberg. In its course, i created my first scientific computer program for the simulation of expansion, reflection and interference of one-dimensional waves.

In the same year, I began my bachelor degree course of computer science at the University of Applied Sciences Augsburg. During the internship semester, I worked as web developer with PHP and JavaScript at gogol publishing. I also participated in an externel project at Linnaeus University, Växjö, Sweden. In winter semester 2014 / 2015, I finished my studies with my bachelor thesis "Generic n-body simulation engine with GPU support".

To complete my education, I will begin a master degree course at the University of Augsburg in summer semester 2015.
