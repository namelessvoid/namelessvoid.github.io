Welcome to my personal website

To figure out more about my career, visit my comprehensive CV . If you want to dive deeper into some of my projects, have a look at the projects page. Also, don't forget to checkout my GitHub and LinkedIn accounts.

I also love animals a lot, therefore you can find me staring at birds or visiting local wild life parks. And finally, on rainy days, I enjoy reading a entertaining book or watching good (and bad) movies at the cinema.
